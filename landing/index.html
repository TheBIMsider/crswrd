How it works

  1. 1Choose your settings (difficulty, pack, tone, grid size).
  2. 2Click Generate Puzzle.
  3. 3Click a white cell and start typing.
  4. 4Use arrow keys to move, click again to switch Across / Down.
  5. 5Use the toolbar to check or reveal when you’re stuck.
Tip: “Everything” mode mixes words from all installed packs for maximum chaos in a good way.

Known limitations

  • Puzzle difficulty can vary due to random generation.
  • No puzzle saving or resume yet.
  • No zoom or pan for large grids (planned).
  • Mobile works, but desktop is the smoothest experience.
